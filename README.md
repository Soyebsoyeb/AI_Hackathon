🌟 Sahayak: Transforming Education in Diverse Indian Classrooms 🇮🇳
Sahayak empowers teachers with intelligent, AI-powered tools tailored for India’s multi-grade classrooms (MGCs), where one teacher handles multiple grades together. It simplifies the creation of:

📝 Customized lesson plans
📚 Grade-specific worksheets
📖 Culturally rich stories
💡 Student-friendly explanations
🌐 All in 12+ Indian languages!

⚙️ Powered by Gemini AI, Sahayak Offers:
📍 Localized Content: Poems, activities, and explanations in regional languages
📈 Adaptive Learning: Worksheets & plans for Grades 1–8
🖼️ Visual Teaching Aids: Auto-generated diagrams, flowcharts & infographics
🧠 Interactive Assistant: ChatGPT-style help for teaching queries
📊 Performance Analytics: Track classroom progress and teaching impact

✅ Perfect for rural and urban schools
✅ Saves teacher prep time ⏱️
✅ Enhances student engagement 🎯
✅ Democratizes quality education 🌍
